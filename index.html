Do's and Don'ts

Will do:

  • Character Design

    Character design of any race, age, gender, or bodytype is certainly possible. They can be drawn in however way you want them to be, meaning whatever pose or angle you want.

  • Illustration

    We specialize in doing full illustrations in whatever style you please as long as it adheres to the rules.

  • Comic Strips

    Whether you wrote a script or we write one for you based off of a few descriptions you can provide, we can do draw it for you.

  • Logos We can do icons or logos but please do ask for permission from us before using it for commercial use.

Won't do:

  • NSFW

    We will not be drawing anything pornographic. We can do things that can be suggestive however we will not do anything considered pornographic.

  • Political Agendas/Bigotry

    We do not want to be involved in the creation of anything that is intended to cause harm to others.

  • Excessive Violence or Gore

    We will not be doing detailed scenes of extreme violence or gore that may be considered ditressing. Implications of extreme violence and mild gore is fine.

  • Copyright Infringement

    Please have a written permission from us before using our works for commercial use. We respect the artist's work and will not tolerate copyright infringement.
